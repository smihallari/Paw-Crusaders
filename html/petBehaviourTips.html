Common Pet Behavior

  • Eating and Drinking

    Common behavior involves regular eating and drinking habits, indicating a healthy appetite and hydration.

  • Playfulness

    Common behavior includes engaging in playful activities, which are essential for mental stimulation and physical exercise.

  • Sleeping Patterns

    Normal sleeping patterns vary based on the pet's age but generally involve consistent and restful sleep, contributing to overall well-being

vs

Anomalous Pet Behavior

  • Changes in Eating Habits

    Anomalous behavior may manifest as sudden changes in eating habits, such as loss of appetite or excessive thirst, which could indicate underlying health issues.

  • Excessive Lethargy

    Unexplained lethargy or lack of interest in activities may be anomalous, suggesting illness, pain, or emotional distress.

  • Altered Sleeping Patterns

    Anomalous sleeping patterns, such as insomnia or excessive sleep, may signal stress, discomfort, or health problems that require attention.
