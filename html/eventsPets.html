Events For Pets

Pet-Friendly Movie Night

Arrange a comfortable space with pillows and blankets, then turn on a pet-friendly film or video. Certain videos are made with appealing images and noises in mind to draw in pets.

Make your own toys:

Take up the craft of making your own toys. Use resources like cardboard, old t-shirts, and socks to make toys. Make it rewarding and enjoyable by hiding snacks within these toys.

Pet-friendly Parties:

Throw a party for your pet to celebrate milestones like their birthday. Bring in other animals and their owners, and have games and snacks that are suitable for animals.

Training Sessions:

Take advantage of the opportunity to train while playtime. Remind your pet of already learned commands or teach them new ones. Their general health benefits from this mental stimulation.
